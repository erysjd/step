Programming Background

The reason why I started programming was so that I did not have to take another History class where all I was going to do was write essays. In my Sophomore year of high school, my school offered their first Computer Science course that taught by our IT guy. Although I did not like the way he taught the class, I enjoyed the material. After Sophomore year, my school decided to continue to offer higher and higher CS courses, even if there were only 5 people in the class. From then on, I decided to major in Computer Science at Fisk University, where I started to fall in love with CS.
